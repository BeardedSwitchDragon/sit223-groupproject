SIT223-SIT753 - Professional Practice in IT

Dear Students, In this unit, you will learn, practice, and try professional concepts that will help you with your future employment success. We will practice concepts around soft skills including teamwork, communications and professional ethics, as well as familiarise ourselves with Information Technology working practices including DevOps, Agile environments, Git version control, and IP.To get things started, you need to do the following before the first lecture: Read the assessment details. Assessment in SIT223-SIT753 is based on a 100% portfolio. Videos on how your assessment for this unit is to be conducted are available in the Introduction module in Week 1. Complete the Week 1 modules before coming to the class. In this unit, you will be attending your weekly active classes (2 hours). We have on-campus classes for students who are enrolled in on-campus mode. We also have online classes for students who are enrolled in online mode and will be using the ZOOM to conduct online classes. The online sessions for units SIT223 and SIT753 are separated. Your assessment of this unit will be based on a 100% learning portfolio. We use OnTrack to submit your assessment tasks throughout the trimester and create the learning portfolio at the end. Login to OnTrack (use your Deakin credentials to login) and check out the Week 1 assessment tasks. Let me know if your login doesn't work.
